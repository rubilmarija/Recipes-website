Gluten free lady fingers recipe

INGREDIENTS:

  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 3 egg whites
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 3/4 cup (110 g) gluten free flour
  • 1 tbs corn flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp xanthan gum

PREPARATION:

  • Half sugar measure combine with egg yolks. Mix until sugar is disolved. Add vanilla and mix it together.Set aside.
  • Beat egg whites and add in the rest of the sugar.
  • Gently fold in egg whites in the egg yolk mixture.
  • Sift gluten free flour, corn flour, baking powder and xanthan gum through the siver into the batter. Gently mix together.
  • Fill the batter into a piping bag.
  • Pipe the batter in small logs on a baking tray.
  • Sprinkle some icing sugar on top.
  • Bake on 180°C for 15 minutes.
